Visit Niagara Falls

The attractions of Niagara Falls, Canada, offer you an authentic Ontario experience. Take a morning cruise in Niagara City and enjoy the Whirlpool Aero Car in the afternoon. Stroll through the botanical gardens or test your courage on the WildPlay Zipline to the Falls. Visit the Skylon Tower for a breathtaking 360-degree view. The Greg Frewin Theatre magic show will amaze both young and old. Awaken the thrill seeker in you with Whirlpool Jetboats, Nightmares Fear Factory, or Niagara Free Fall. There are so many things to do in Niagara Falls for fun and adventure!
