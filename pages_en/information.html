FAQ

What are the cruise schedules?
Cruises start at 8:00 AM and end at 9:00 PM every day.
Where can we eat?
Several restaurants are available on site, near the observation points.
How to get to the falls from Toronto?
By car, about 1h30; by bus or guided tour, several options are available.
